# AirPlay Receiver

Back in the day, flagship phones used to have an AUX port, with which it was easy to connect Hi-Fi audio systems and stream, nay, simpy just play music.

<br />

When I switched my iPhone SE (1st gen) to my iPhone 12 Mini, it occured to me that I would no longer be able to use my sound system that easily, because it no longer had said AUX port...

<br />

This was the time when my father introduced me to the concept of IoT and with the help of a handy Raspberry Pi 1, which of course had a very basic AUX port, after some research, I could install a dockerized version of an AirPlay receiver.

<br />

From this point on, I could again use my speakers and enjoy listening to my music without ear/headphones!
